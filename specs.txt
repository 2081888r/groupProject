    Presentation of Specifications and Design 5% (due in 21/2 in lab)
    
    Your team will need to take the design brief and translate it into a set of specifications,
    providing an idea of the users of the application, and what functionality is relevant to the
    different types of users. Your team will need to construct the overall system architecture,
    the ER diagram and the wireframes and walkthrough of the system being used.
    
    Project demonstration 10% (due in lab 21/3)
    
    Your team will need to demonstrate the application that your team has built. 
    It should be professional clear and concise. It should focus on explaining the different
    features and how the brief has been met.
    
    Application Code 25% (due on the 21/3)
    
    You will need to submit a link the repository where the code is stored, a link to the
    deployed application, the revised design artifacts should also be re-submitted.
